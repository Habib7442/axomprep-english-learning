## 🔑 Conversion Principles Applied

- **Zero friction CTA** — "Free" is in every button, no card required
- **Debate Mode badge** — creates curiosity, drives signups just to try it
- **Demo video** — show don't tell, voice products MUST be heard/seen
- **Specificity** — "5 min/day", "100 pages", "2 hours" build trust
